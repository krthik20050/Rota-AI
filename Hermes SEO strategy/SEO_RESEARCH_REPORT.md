# ROTA AI — SEO RESEARCH REPORT

## Executive Summary

Rota AI is a free, open source voice dictation app for Windows. One developer (Karthik Krishnan, student in Kerala, India). MIT license. No funding, no team, no marketing budget.

This document outlines the SEO strategy to get Rota AI discovered by people searching for voice dictation tools, Wispr Flow alternatives, and free dictation software.

## Target Audience

1. **Primary**: Windows users who want free voice dictation (students, developers, budget-conscious professionals)
2. **Secondary**: People searching for Wispr Flow alternatives (hit the payment wall)
3. **Tertiary**: Privacy-conscious users who want offline voice dictation
4. **Developer niche**: Developers who want voice coding (Cursor, VS Code)

## Competitor Analysis

### Wispr Flow
- $15/month, $180/year
- Mac, Windows, iPhone, Android
- $81M raised
- Strong brand, polished product
- Weakness: price, no offline mode, cloud-only

### SuperWhisper
- $8.49/month
- Mac, Windows, iOS
- Popular with developers (Karpathy endorsement)
- Weakness: not free, not open source, no Android

### Otter.ai
- Free tier: 300 min/month
- Web, iOS, Android
- Transcription tool, not dictation tool
- Weakness: not real-time dictation, privacy concerns

### Windows Built-In Dictation
- Free, pre-installed
- No AI cleanup, no context awareness
- Weakness: raw transcription only

## Content Strategy

### Phase 1: Foundation (Week 1-2)
- Launch website with homepage, install page, comparison page
- Set up technical SEO (meta tags, schema, sitemap, robots.txt)
- Publish 1 blog post: origin story

### Phase 2: Content Engine (Week 3-8)
- Publish 2 blog posts per week
- Focus on high-priority comparison and how-to posts
- Each post must be edited to sound human (not AI-generated)

### Phase 3: Sustained Growth (Month 3+)
- 1-2 posts per week based on user questions
- Build backlinks through genuine community participation
- Update old posts every 2-3 months

## GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)

To get cited by ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini:
1. Use clear, quotable statements
2. Include specific data points and comparisons
3. Write in a conversational tone (AI tools prefer citing conversational content)
4. Add structured data (FAQ schema, Article schema)
5. Create comparison tables (AI tools love extracting tables)
6. Be the original source of unique information (personal experience counts)
